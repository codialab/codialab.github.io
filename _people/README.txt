People page format
==================

The file name has to be

FirstLetterOfFirstNameFamilyName.md

The content has to be formatted as follows:

---
name: John William Doe
pub-names:
- Doe J
- Doe JW
degree: academic degree
email: preferred email
homepage: personal homepage [full URL]
scholar: Google scholar link [full URL]
github: your personal github name [no URL]
orcid: your ORCID [no URL]
twitter: your personal twitter handle [no URL]
role: your role in the Marschall Lab
institutes:
- list of institutes (short names) you are affiliated with
portrait: /img/jdoe.jpg
bio: short bio
alum: false
---
